## 🔧 HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

### Step-by-Step:
1. **Fill in all placeholders** above with your actual content
2. **Gather all required images/videos** listed
3. **Review for brand voice consistency** (luxury, energetic, friendly)
4. **Have a second person proofread** all copy
5. **Update the HTML wireframe** with your content
6. **Test all links and CTAs** before going live

### Tips for Great Copy:
- ✅ Keep headlines under 10 words when possible
- ✅ Use active voice ("Transform your performance" not "Your performance will be transformed")
- ✅ Include specific numbers and metrics for credibility
- ✅ Focus on benefits, not just features
- ✅ Address objections proactively
- ✅ Maintain consistent tone throughout
- ✅ Use power words: Elite, Dominate, Unleash, Transform, Accelerate

### Questions to Ask Before Finalizing:
- Does every headline pass the "Grunt Test"? (Clear what you offer in 5 seconds)
- Is every CTA action-oriented and specific?
- Do your authority stats have proof/sources?
- Are objections truly the top ones your prospects have?
- Does the copy sound like how you'd talk to a client?
