Kelly Parker

MSN, APRN-CNP | Founder & Clinical Lead

Kelly Parker didn't choose functional medicine because it was popular. She chose it because it was the only thing that finally helped when the system she trusted couldn't.

As a Family Nurse Practitioner in traditional primary care, Kelly believed in the model she was trained in. She followed the rules, trusted the research, and showed up fully for her patients. In 2020, she recovered from COVID and expected to move forward with life as usual. Instead, after receiving the COVID vaccine soon afterward, her health began to spiral in ways she never anticipated.

Her fevers reached 104°. Migraines became relentless. Brain fog stole her clarity. Fatigue hit so hard she questioned how she would keep practicing. Walking upstairs left her winded. Thinking between patients felt like pushing through mud. The provider who once reassured others now found herself searching for reassurance.

She did everything she was taught to do.
Specialists. MRIs. Neurology consults. Standard labs.
Most results came back "normal."
But her life was no longer normal.

Sitting on the other side of the exam table feeling dismissed, unheard, and reduced to a lab value lit a fire in her that never went out. She realized how many patients must feel the same quiet frustration when their symptoms don't fit neatly into a box.

Out of equal parts desperation and determination, Kelly turned to functional medicine. Not as a rebellion against conventional care, but as a deeper search for answers. Through comprehensive testing and targeted support for inflammation, nutrient depletion, gut health, and stress physiology, her body began to respond.

Within 90 days, she felt hope. By nine months, she had her life back.

The tools weren't extreme. They were foundational: vitamin D, magnesium, anti-inflammatory support, real nourishment, sleep, and restoring metabolic balance. Simple, yet powerful when personalized and applied with intention.

That experience didn't just heal her. It reshaped her.

Today, Kelly leads Functional Health Warriors for people who refuse to accept "you're fine" when they don't feel fine. She looks at root causes, optimal ranges, and the full story behind the labs because symptoms are signals, not inconveniences. She doesn't reject conventional medicine, she expands on it. She believes acute care saves lives, but true healing requires time, curiosity, and partnership.

Her mission is to help people feel strong in their bodies again, to trust their signals, and to reclaim ownership of their health.

Kelly Parker is a Family Nurse Practitioner, founder of Functional Health Warriors, and a mom of two who specializes in metabolic health, adrenal resilience, thyroid optimization, hormone balance, and peptide therapies.
