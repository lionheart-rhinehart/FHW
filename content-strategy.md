**OBJECTION #1:** "I already have a trainer/coach."
**RESPONSE:** "That's great—we complement your existing coaching by focusing specifically on the biomechanical and performance aspects that most sport coaches don't have time to address. Many of our clients are referred by their head coaches who want their athletes to have that extra edge."

**OBJECTION #2:** "This seems expensive for training."

We've helped athletes earn scholarships worth hundreds of thousands of dollars by improving their measurable performance metrics."

**OBJECTION #3:** "I don't know if I'm athletic enough for this type of training."
**RESPONSE:** "Our programs are designed for athletes at every level—from youth development to professional preparation. We meet you where you are and build from there. Some of our biggest success stories started with athletes who didn't think they were 'elite material.'"

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Language Style:
- **Powerful but not aggressive**: Confident, aspirational
- **Scientific but accessible**: Data-driven without jargon
- **Exclusive but welcoming**: Premium positioning, inclusive tone
- **Results-focused**: Measurable outcomes, proof points

### Words to Use:
Elite, unleash, dominate, breakthrough, optimize, accelerate, precision, scientific, personalized, proven, competitive edge, peak performance

### Words to Avoid:
Cheap, easy, basic, amateur, everyone, generic, simple

---

## Color & Design Notes

### Suggested Color Palette:
- **Primary**: Deep Navy Blue (luxury, trust, power)
- **Accent**: Electric Blue or Vibrant Orange (energy, movement)
- **Neutral**: Crisp White, Charcoal Gray (clean, professional)
- **Highlight**: Metallic Silver accents (premium, high-tech)

### Design Aesthetic:
- Clean, modern layouts with ample white space
- High-quality action photography
- Bold typography with strong hierarchy
- Subtle motion/animation on scroll
- Professional but energetic feel
